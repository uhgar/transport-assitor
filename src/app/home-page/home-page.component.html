Why T-Log

T-Log creators has experience of operating big transport fleet in the past, With that knowledge we have crafted an application with which you can get to know the overview of the performance of your business.



How T-Log Works

T-Log saves the day-to day logs of your vehicles entered by users such as Income, Expense, Active or Inactive...etc. It also reminds you of your vechicles next Fitness Check, Insurance Expiry, age of your vehicles. So, with these data T-Log helps you to see Income Vs Expense -> Profit/Loss for an independent vehicle, Based on this information from T-Log it's simple for you to make changes in your Business and make effective use of your Tranport fleet.
